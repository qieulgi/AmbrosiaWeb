Menu

Appetizers

Briam – A medley of thinly sliced vegetables, delicately seasoned with oregano, rosemary, parsley, and fresh garlic, then drizzled with extra virgin olive oil.

Soutzoukakia – Savory ground beef blended with onion, garlic, fresh herbs, and aromatic cumin, slowly simmered in a rich tomato sauce.

Bouyiourdi – Oven-baked feta cheese with ripe tomatoes, garlic, and chili, finished with extra virgin olive oil and served with warm pita bread.

House Specialty: Chimera Platter – A divine trio of dolmas, grilled octopus, and roasted red pepper hummus served with warm lavash.

Entrées

Branzino – Whole Greek sea bass, expertly prepared and infused with fresh lemon wedges, oregano, and rosemary, drizzled with olive oil for a delicate, flavorful finish.

Moussaka – A traditional Greek dish featuring layers of roasted eggplant and a rich, spiced lamb sauce, topped with a velvety béchamel.

Lahanorizio – A comforting braise of sautéed cabbage, fresh vegetables, and creamy rice, brought to perfection with a splash of lemon juice, a hint of parsley, and crumbles of feta cheese.

House Specialty: Poseidon's Feast – A grilled seafood medley of shrimp, calamari, and scallops, kissed by citrus and herbs.

Desserts

Baklava Ice Cream Sundae – Layers of crispy, golden phyllo pastry combined with rich vanilla ice cream, topped with cinnamon-dusted pistachios and walnuts, then finished with a warm drizzle of honey.

Loukoumades – Traditional Greek honey puffs, lightly fried to golden perfection, dusted with crushed nuts, and served with a side of fragrant honey.

Portokalopita – A luscious, butter-free orange custard cake crafted with phyllo dough and bathed in a fragrant honey-orange syrup.

House Specialty: Nectar of the Gods – Rosewater panna cotta topped with candied figs and a drizzle of saffron honey.
